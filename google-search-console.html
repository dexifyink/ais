Google Search Console Setup for DEX BYPASS

Follow these steps to set up Google Search Console for your website. This is crucial for getting your site indexed by Google and tracking its performance in search results.

Step 1: Access Google Search Console

Go to Google Search Console and sign in with your Google account.

Step 2: Add Your Property

Click on "Add Property" and select "URL prefix" as the property type.

Enter your website URL: https://dexbypass.net/

Step 3: Verify Ownership

You'll need to verify that you own the website. Google offers several verification methods:

  1. HTML file upload: Download the HTML file provided by Google and upload it to your website's root directory.
  2. HTML tag: Add the meta tag provided by Google to the section of your homepage.
  3. DNS record: Add the TXT record provided by Google to your domain's DNS configuration.
  4. Google Analytics: If you already have Google Analytics set up, you can use this method.
  5. Google Tag Manager: If you use Google Tag Manager, you can verify through it.

The HTML tag method is often the easiest. Copy the meta tag provided by Google and add it to the section of your index.html file.

Note: After adding the verification method, click "Verify" in Google Search Console. It may take a few moments to verify.

Step 4: Submit Your Sitemap

Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left sidebar.

Enter sitemap.xml in the field and click "Submit".

Note: Make sure your sitemap.xml file is accessible at https://dexbypass.net/sitemap.xml before submitting.

Step 5: Request Indexing

To speed up the indexing process:

  1. Go to "URL Inspection" in the left sidebar.
  2. Enter your homepage URL: https://dexbypass.net/
  3. Click "Request Indexing".

Step 6: Monitor Performance

Check back regularly to monitor:

  • Coverage: Ensure your pages are being indexed without errors.
  • Performance: Track how your site appears in search results.
  • Enhancements: Check for any enhancement opportunities.
  • Manual Actions: Ensure your site hasn't received any penalties.

Step 7: Set Up Search Appearance

In the "Search Appearance" section, you can see how your site appears in search results and make improvements if needed.

Important: It may take several days or even weeks for Google to fully index your site after submission. Be patient and check back regularly.
